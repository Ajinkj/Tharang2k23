Eureka 4.0 | Idea Pitching Competition.

Guidelines:

  1. Eligibility: The competition is open to all students who have an interest in Emerging Technologies. Students can participate individually or in teams of up to three members.
  2. Theme: The theme of the competition is "Mechatronics." All ideas should be related to this theme.
  3. Format: The competition will be held in two rounds. In the first round, participants will submit their ideas in the form of a Idea abstract or pitch deck. In the second round, shortlisted participants will present their ideas in front of a panel of judges.
  4. Idea Abstract: The proposal should include a clear description of the idea, its potential impact, and the feasibility of its implementation. The proposal should also include any relevant sketches or diagrams.
  5. Presentation: Shortlisted participants will be given a time limit to present their ideas in front of the judges. The presentation should include a clear description of the idea, its potential impact, and the feasibility of its implementation. The presentation should also include any relevant visual aids or prototypes.
  6. Judging criteria: The proposals and presentations will be judged based on the following criteria:
    • Creativity and originality
    • Feasibility of implementation
    • Potential impact and relevance to the theme
    • Clarity of presentation
  7. Prizes: Prizes will be awarded to the winners, and their ideas may also be considered for further development or implementation.
  8. Submission deadline: The deadline for submitting proposals will be announced in advance.
